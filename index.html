EfficientNet-B0 backbone, two independent classification heads, trained jointly with test-time augmentation · Source and training notebook
What this model can't do. It only knows the seven species it was trained on, and has no "not a mosquito" category. Predictions below 50% confidence are reported as unreliable rather than shown as a species — a useful heuristic, but not true out-of-distribution detection. Sibling species within the Anopheles gambiae complex remain the hardest to separate, as they are for human entomologists.
